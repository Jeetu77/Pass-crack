Information Entropy

Calculated as E = L × log2(R). This measures the mathematical unpredictability of your password based on its length (L) and the pool of possible characters (R).

Pattern Predictability

True entropy assumes randomness. Dictionary words or keyboard walks (like "qwerty" or "123456") drastically reduce the complexity attackers need to guess your password.
